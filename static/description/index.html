Website Appointment Booking in odoo

Website Appointment Booking is a dynamic appointment booking module in odoo allows you to accept online date and time bookings from customers, consultants and accept booking orders. Date booking and Slot booking options are available for booking in this app. You can send Pending Booking Notification to customers and control from admin. Send Booking confirmation email Notification or cancellation Notification to the customers.

Take your customers appointments online with this module and manage your time. Manage appointments in odoo, this Website Appointment booking module supported version 15, 14, 13, 12. Website appointment booking system in odoo can set up appointments for your customers in the most effective way with the help of the Appointment slot booking odoo apps. This app works for SPA, Car services booking, website booking, Beauty business and any other business who want to take appointment online.

Website appointment booking in odoo module available in Odoo V15, V14, V13, V12 versions with Multiple languages supported (English, Arabic, Danish-dansk, Dutch-Nederlands, Spanish - Español, French-français) in odoo backend.
